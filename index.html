    About Us

    Going Green with EV

    AZoom Car Rental

    AZoom Car Rental specializes in providing electric car rentals to the general public. We have an appealing selection of electric vehicles to meet a variety of needs and occasions for our customers. Customers who are concerned about the environment.

    By choosing to drive an EV, you are helping to reduce harmful air pollution caused by exhaust emissions. An electric car emits no greenhouse gases because its motor is powered by a battery rather than burning fuel. It also necessitates fewer of the more hazardous, contaminating liquids such as engine oil and coolant fluids.
